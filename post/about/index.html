About Me

2020-06-23

Hi! I am “Robert” Chen Bao. I am a first-year undergrad at UVa.

Born in China, I came to the US in 2016. I spent my four years of high school in Fort Myers, Florida. I have a lot of experience doing science fairs during high school. I also actively participated in community service events. In my senior year, I served as the secretary of the National Honor Society.

I plan to major in Computer Science, and I am deeply interested in cloud computing and data analytics. I had two years of experience writing Python programs for my data science projects. I created Twitter Analytic Program, a Business Intelligence dashboard. With this project, I won first place in the twelfth-grade division as well as Best in Fair at Thomas A. Edison Regional Inventor Fairs. I also earned admission to the Florida State Science and Engineering Fair (SSEF) for two consecutive years.

In my spare time, I enjoy reading, editing photos, and watching movies. Some of my favorites are Kiki's Delivery Service by Studio Ghibli and Moonrise Kingdom by Wes Anderson.
