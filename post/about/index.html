About

2020-06-24

Hi! I am “Robert” Chen Bao. I am a Computer Science undergrad at University of Virginia.

Born in China, I came to the US in 2016 and spent my 4 years of high school in Fort Myers, Florida. At school, I spent lots of time doing science fair and doing community service (besides doing my schoolwork). I helped at summer camp often, teaching young kids coding and Photoshopping.

As a CS major, I am interested in web development and data analysis. I have been writing programs for research for three years in high school. In 2019, I wrote a Business Intelligence dashboard titled Twitter Analytic Program for the regional inventors fair. With this invention, I won first place in the twelfth-grade division as well as Best in Fair.

In my spare time, I like reading and editing photos, and researching productivity tricks. I am also a big fan of Studio Ghibli movies. (My favorite movie is Kiki's Delivery Service.) My favorite artist is Jonas Brothers.
