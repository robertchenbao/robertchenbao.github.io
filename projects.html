Projects

  • Using Dominating Sets to Block Contagions in Social Networks: A research paper about blocking contagions (e.g., virus, emotions, and misinformation) in a population. It was accepted to the ASONAM conference in the fall of 2022.
  • GlucOverwatch: A Python program that monitors glucose levels of Type-1 diabetes patients. It was the winner of UVA's Hack4Health 2022's Best Health Hack track.
  • Twitter Analytic Program: A machine-learning web app that can analyze and visualize over 20,000 tweets. Winner of Fort Myer's Thomas A. Edison Inventor Fair in 2020
  • Marketing from Media: A data science project on Twitter. I mined a 3.5 GB tweet dataset to analyze public perception on Tesla, and its connection to Tesla car sales/stock price. It won first place at Fort Myer's Thomas A. Edison Science Fair in both 2019 and 2020, and earned admission to Florida's state-wide competition.
